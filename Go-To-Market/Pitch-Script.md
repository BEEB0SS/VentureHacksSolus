## 60-Second Version
Robotics teams use 6+ disconnected tools — KiCad for PCBs, Onshape for CAD, ROS for middleware, GitHub for code. No tool understands the robot as a full system. When something changes in hardware, nobody knows what breaks in software until it breaks.

Solus fixes this with a Robotics Context Model — a living graph that ingests your design files, code, telemetry, and simulation state into one unified context. Swap a chip on your PCB, and Solus instantly shows which software modules are impacted and explains why. Stream live telemetry, and Solus detects anomalies and diagnoses them using your full system context and your team's past knowledge.

It's Cursor for robotics — not replacing your tools, but finally connecting them.
