# Target Audience

> Part of [[Vision]]

## Primary User
**Robotics engineer** (research lab / startup / competition team) who works across hardware + software, uses multiple disconnected tools, and spends more time debugging than building.

### Profile
- Uses ROS, KiCad, Onshape, GitHub, and a mix of other tools daily
- Works on embedded systems, mechanical design, AND software
- Part of a small team (2-10 people) where everyone wears multiple hats
- Frustrated by integration overhead and lack of system-level visibility
- Values tools that augment their thinking, not replace it

### Where They Are
- CMU Robotics Institute and similar research labs
- University competition teams (FIRST, RoboCup, autonomous vehicle)
- Early-stage robotics startups
- R&D departments at larger companies

## Secondary User
**Technical founder / team lead** who manages system architecture, onboards new engineers, and wants faster iteration and team productivity.

### What They Care About
- Reducing onboarding time for new team members
- Preserving institutional knowledge when people leave
- Understanding cross-domain impact of changes before they break things
- Faster iteration cycles from design to test
